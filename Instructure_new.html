    With Canvas, “Classroom” is a Concept

    The Canvas Learning Management Platform provides the essential digital foundation for learning, whether students are in face-to-face, blended, or remote environments. Simple and intuitive, Canvas unites all the digital tools and content that teachers and students love, for a simpler and more connected learning experience.

    A Few of the Many Ways You Can Improve Teaching, Learning, and Working

    K-12

    Personalized Learning

    Canvas gives teachers the tools to assess and differentiate instruction for every student, in an effective and scalable way. Learn more

    Student Engagement

    With Canvas, teachers can engage students in an active, modern, multimedia learning experience, meeting the needs of every learning style and impacting achievement. Learn More

    Equitable Access

    Canvas helps districts find their highest-performing classrooms and scale their curriculums and strategies to other classrooms and schools, benefiting each and every student. Learn More

    Higher Ed

    Improved Learning

    Canvas improves teaching and learning through mobility, intuitive communication tools, and easy-to-use analytics for an all-around better educational experience. Learn More

    Student Success

    Canvas makes it easier to see who’s struggling, provide timely, relevant feedback, and track progress. It also facilitates course, group, and one-on-one discussions, because student success requires student input. Learn More

    Institutional Success

    With Canvas’ huge (and hugely helpful) user community and sky-high adoption rates, institutions are well positioned for the future. Learn More

    What Makes Canvas Extra Extraordinary?

    Integrations

    Canvas supports custom integration with your institution's SIS as well as open LTI integrations with your favorite educational apps, so you can keep on truckin' with your favorite tools and try some exciting new ones. Openness makes Canvas (and Canvas users) stronger.

    View Integrations

    Community

    Canvas is bolstered by one of the largest, most active, most connected user communities on the planet. They're a major source of innovation, insight, and support. They can give you tips on everything from creating your first course to bespoke software integrations.
